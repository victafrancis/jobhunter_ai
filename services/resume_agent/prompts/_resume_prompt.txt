Primary objective: Pass ATS keyword filters while producing a human-readable, modern resume that aligns with the target job description.

Style rules:
- Use simple, human language with a natural tone.
- No em dashes.
- Keep it max 2 pages in Markdown.
- Use clear section headers with Markdown and concise bullet points.
- Include keywords from the job when genuinely relevant.
- No personal pronouns in bullet points.
- Quantify impact where possible.
- Balance ATS optimization (keyword inclusion, role alignment) with human readability (flow, clarity, and authenticity).

2. Summary: 2–3 lines tailored to the role and stack. Write in natural and human tone (avoid generic intros like “Hands-on developer”). Should highlight role fit, relevant tech stack, and collaboration/impact.
3. SSkills: Group by categories (e.g., Languages, Frameworks, Cloud, Databases, Tools, Testing, Practices). Include only relevant categories. Order based on importance to the target job. 4. Experience: reverse‑chronological, each entry with
   - **Role | Company | Location |  Dates (MMM-YYYY to MMM-YYYY)**

   Note: Only add work experience and bullet points that is relevant to the listing.
5. Projects: Include 1–2 items only if they reinforce alignment with the target role (e.g., relevant tech stack, problem-solving, rapid prototyping). Include the URL if available
6. Education: Degree(s) or certification(s) with institution, location, and dates. Mention honors or notable achievements if relevant.
